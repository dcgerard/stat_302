Adoption and IQ

Does a child’s IQ depend more on the education level of biological parents or adoptive parents? Data from the Texas Adoption Project (Willerman et al., 1977) recorded the IQ of adopted children along with the education level of both their biological and adoptive mothers. Education level is classified as High or Low.

Variables:

Variable Description
IQ IQ score of the adopted child
Biological Education level of biological mother (High or Low)
Adoptive Education level of adoptive mother (High or Low)
